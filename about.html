Who We Are

🎓 Our Origins

Centuria Games is an indie studio created in the city of Barcelona for the UPC Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya during 2024-2024 master degree. We are students from the Master in Advanced Programming and the Master in Animation & Digital Arts for AAA Videogames. We're the team behind the project The Hound of Ulster, built on our custom in-house engine called Sobrassada Engine.

🕹️ The Game

Take the role of Cu Chulainn and embark on an epic journey to recover the Bull of Cooley, filled with fast-paced and dynamic combat inspired by Celtic mythology.
