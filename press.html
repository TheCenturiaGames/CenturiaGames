Description

The Hound of Ulster is a top-down 3D action RPG inspired by the legendary Irish myth, The Ulster Cycle. Engage in fast-paced hack-and-slash combat against a variety of enemies across mystical landscapes brimming with history, traps, and things waiting to be broken. Attack, dash, heal, and transform as you carve your path through each level and uncover the destiny that awaits you.

Story

Long after mortals stole the land of the gods, conflict stirs in ancient Ireland… Embark on a fated journey as Cú Chulainn, the legendary Hound of Ulster. Reclaim the stolen Mystical Brown Bull and restore Ulster’s lost glory. Haunted by The Morrígan, goddess of war and fate, you will face battles that test your strength, your spirit, and destiny itself. Will you face the cost of prophesied greatness?

About Centuria Games

Centuria Games is an indie game studio formed within the UPC (Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya), as part of the master’s degree in "Advanced Programming and Animation & Digital Arts for AAA Video Games."
